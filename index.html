ABOUT US

TBB Trading company was established in Dubai city, UAE as agricultural and wood commodity wholesale trader with high level of customers service from around the globe, by year later our company launched representative office in Ukraine, Odessa. Since foundation our company became one of the leading exporter of sawn timber from Ukraine with a market share of over 25% and we are increasing this figure of market share from year by year.

Today our company owns more than 8000 hectares of land and own storage facilities over 10,000 square meters with 5 hectares of the adjacent territory located in Odessa at a distance of up to 3 km. to the largest seaport in Ukraine and 8 highly equipped sawmills.

Besides our own production, our company purchase different agricultural products directly from Ukrainian farmers all year round, which allows us to fully control the quality and ensure uninterrupted supply of goods in large volumes for our customers

Today the activities of our company has spread to more than 40 countries and 80 companies around the world with a monthly turnover more than 700 containers of timber and agricultural products, and we are increasing this figure from year to year.

1. Farming department - with land bank more than 8000 hectares;

3. Trading department – in 2019 our main market was China but for today our activity reached to more than 10 others countries like: UAE, India, Israel, Korea, Vietnam etc.

All our business activity happens according GAFTA rules and under control of independent survey company GAFTA member.

Purchase

Due to our well-deserved reputation as a reliable and responsible buyer, we have constant and reliable relationships with Ukrainian manufacturers that ensure uninterrupted supplies of products all year round.

Warehouse delivery

One of the important pricing components are logistics costs and costs associated with warehouse work, due to the fact that our company owns warehouse premises of more than 10,000 square meters, we are more reliable and competitively advantageous than many other companies that often do not have their own warehouse capacities

Special service

All our regular customers are privileged and has special conditions like payment terms, prices, loading conditions and many others services available with us.

Informational support

To provide high level service and be online with all our buyers, our team is available on the calls and e-mail during 24/7. All our buyers will be served and got informational support in max 2 hours from request to response.
